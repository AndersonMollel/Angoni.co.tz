Big Five Comfort Safari - Serengeti & Ngorongoro

5 Days
From $2,500
Serengeti & Ngorongoro
2-6 People

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Transfer to Arusha

Arrive at Kilimanjaro International Airport (JRO), meet your professional guide, and transfer to your luxury lodge in Arusha. Evening orientation and safari briefing.

Day 2-3: Serengeti National Park Game Drives

Experience full-day game drives in the world-famous Serengeti, home to the Great Migration and abundant wildlife including lions, elephants, cheetahs, and over 500 bird species.

Day 4: Ngorongoro Crater Exploration

Descend into the Ngorongoro Crater, a UNESCO World Heritage site, for a full day of wildlife viewing in this natural amphitheater teeming with animals.

Day 5: Departure

Enjoy a final breakfast at your lodge with breathtaking views before transferring back to the airport for your departure flight with unforgettable memories.

Why Choose This Safari?

Our Big Five Comfort Safari is designed for travelers who want to experience Tanzania's most iconic wildlife in comfort and style. You'll have the opportunity to see lions, leopards, elephants, rhinos, and buffalo in their natural habitats.

What Makes This Safari Special?

This safari combines two of Tanzania's most famous parks - the vast plains of the Serengeti and the unique ecosystem of the Ngorongoro Crater. The Serengeti is home to the Great Wildebeest Migration, while the Ngorongoro Crater offers some of the best wildlife viewing in Africa with its high concentration of animals.

Accommodation

You'll stay in carefully selected lodges and tented camps that offer comfort without compromising the authentic safari experience. Each accommodation has been chosen for its location, service, and unique character.

Expert Guides

Our guides are local experts with years of experience and extensive knowledge of Tanzania's wildlife, ecosystems, and cultures. They'll ensure you have the best possible safari experience.
